Professional Skin Care for Real Schedules

The Urban Facial was designed with a simple premise: most people want great skin, but most people also have limited time. Standard 60 or 90-minute facials are a luxury not everyone can fit into a working week, but that doesn't mean skin care has to be sacrificed. The Urban Facial compresses the essential steps of a professional treatment into 40 focused, effective minutes — delivering cleaner pores, brighter skin, and genuine hydration that you can see and feel immediately after the session.

At La Belle SPA in Shoreline, our estheticians perform the Urban Facial with the same quality products and professional touch used in our longer treatments. The session begins with a double cleanse to remove surface impurities and makeup residue, followed by light exfoliation to lift dead skin cells, toning to restore pH balance, targeted serum application, and a finishing moisturizer and SPF layer. There is no filler time here — every step serves the result. Clients frequently walk out surprised by how much difference 40 minutes can make.

The Ideal First Facial — and a Regular Ritual

For clients who have never had a professional facial, the Urban Facial is the perfect introduction. It allows you to experience the difference between professional-grade products and at-home routines, understand how a skilled esthetician reads and addresses your skin's specific needs, and see results without committing significant time or expense. Many of our most loyal clients started with an Urban Facial and now maintain a monthly treatment schedule that combines express and deeper treatments depending on their skin's seasonal needs.

The treatment can also be elevated with optional add-ons: a hand treatment that pairs beautifully with the facial time, or a back massage that transforms a quick visit into a more complete self-care experience. We also offer the Urban Facial as a couple's add-on, making it an excellent pairing for clients who want to bring a partner without requiring both people to commit to a full facial service length.
