Facial-Grade Care for Your Back

Your back has skin too — and it deserves the same level of professional attention as your face. The back facial at La Belle SPA applies the complete facial protocol to your back and décolletage: deep cleansing, steam, manual extractions, a customized exfoliation treatment, a hydrating or purifying mask, and finishing moisturizer. The result is smooth, clear skin in an area most people have never been able to treat properly.

Back acne — sometimes called bacne — is one of the most common and frustrating skin concerns. The skin on the back has a higher density of sebaceous glands than almost anywhere else on the body, which makes it particularly prone to congestion, breakouts, and texture irregularities. At the same time, it's nearly impossible to treat yourself at home. Professional extractions performed by a licensed esthetician reach deep into clogged pores safely, without the scarring risk of at-home picking.

The treatment is also ideal for anyone preparing for a backless dress, wedding gown, or special occasion where the skin of the back and shoulders will be visible. Clients typically notice a visible improvement in smoothness and clarity after a single session, with optimal results after a series of three to four treatments spaced two to four weeks apart.

Who Benefits Most

The back facial is an excellent choice for anyone who struggles with back breakouts, rough texture, or skin congestion in areas they can't address at home. It's also a popular pre-event treatment for brides, prom attendees, and anyone wearing a backless or shoulder-baring outfit. Many clients combine the back facial with a relaxing Swedish massage to create a truly comprehensive full-body wellness session.

We recommend a series of treatments for those dealing with chronic back acne or significant congestion. Your esthetician will assess your skin at the first appointment and recommend an appropriate maintenance schedule to keep your back clear and smooth long-term.
