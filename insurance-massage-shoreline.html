Use Your Health Benefits for Therapeutic Massage

At La Belle SPA, we believe therapeutic massage should be accessible to everyone who needs it. That's why we're proud to be an in-network provider with Premera Blue Cross, one of the Pacific Northwest's most trusted health insurers. If you have massage therapy benefits under your Premera plan, you may be able to receive professional massage treatment at La Belle SPA with little or no out-of-pocket cost.

How It Works

1

Verify Your Coverage

Call your Premera Blue Cross member services or contact us at 425-329-9520 to verify your massage therapy benefits. Coverage varies by plan, and we want to make sure you understand exactly what's included before your appointment.

2

Schedule Your Appointment

Book online or text us to arrange a session. Let us know you'll be using insurance when scheduling so we can prepare the appropriate paperwork and ensure smooth processing of your claim.

3

Bring Your Insurance Card

Bring your Premera Blue Cross card to your first appointment. We'll handle the billing directly, so you can focus entirely on your treatment and recovery.

4

Enjoy Your Session

Receive the same exceptional care as any client — your insurance handles the payment, you enjoy the results. Our licensed therapists deliver the same high-quality therapeutic massage regardless of payment method.

What to Bring to Your First Insurance Appointment

  • Your Premera Blue Cross insurance card (front and back)
  • A doctor's referral or prescription if required by your specific plan
  • Government-issued photo ID
  • Any pre-authorization numbers provided by Premera
  • A list of current medications or health conditions relevant to your treatment

Frequently Asked Questions

Is massage therapy covered by insurance?

Many Premera Blue Cross plans include massage therapy as a covered benefit when it is medically necessary — meaning it is prescribed or recommended by a licensed physician for the treatment of a specific condition such as chronic pain, muscle injury, stress-related illness, or post-surgical recovery. Purely relaxation-focused massage may not be covered, but therapeutic massage for medical conditions often is. The best way to know for certain is to call the member services number on the back of your insurance card before your appointment.

How many massage sessions are covered?

The number of covered sessions varies significantly by plan. Some Premera plans cover a set number of visits per year (often 12–20), while others may cover massage as part of a broader physical therapy or rehabilitation benefit. We will help you understand your specific coverage when you call or visit for the first time, and we can help you maximize your benefits throughout the year.

Do I need a doctor's referral?

Whether a referral is required depends on your specific Premera plan. Some plans allow direct access to massage therapy providers, while others require a physician's prescription or referral. We strongly recommend calling Premera member services before your appointment to clarify requirements for your plan — this ensures there are no surprises at billing time. Our staff is happy to help you navigate the process.
