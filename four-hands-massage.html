What is a Four Hands Massage?

A four hands massage is the most indulgent single-person massage experience available. Two skilled therapists work in perfect choreographic synchrony — mirror movements, complementary strokes, and coordinated pressure — covering your entire body simultaneously in ways no single therapist can achieve.

The effect on the nervous system is extraordinary. When your brain receives simultaneous sensory input from both sides of your body and from opposite ends (head and feet at the same time, for example), it becomes impossible to maintain the analytical mental chatter that prevents deep relaxation. The result is a meditative state that experienced meditators work for years to achieve.

Our four hands sessions are choreographed by two of our senior therapists who have practiced this synchronized technique together. The result is seamless — you'll barely believe there are two people working, until the profound depth of relaxation tells you otherwise.
