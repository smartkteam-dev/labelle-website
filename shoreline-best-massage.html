Our Mission

Elevating Wellness in the Shoreline Community

La Belle SPA was founded on a singular conviction: that exceptional therapeutic care should be available to everyone in Shoreline and the surrounding community — not just those with the time and budget for destination resort spas. We set out to build a space where luxury and effectiveness coexist, where every client walks out feeling genuinely better than when they walked in.

We are proud to serve the Shoreline community from our suite at 18820 Aurora Ave N, just minutes from Shoreline, Kenmore, Mountlake Terrace, and Lynnwood. We accept Premera Blue Cross insurance, making therapeutic massage more accessible for those who need it most.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

01

Expertise

Every therapist at La Belle SPA holds a current Washington State massage therapy license and brings years of specialized training across multiple modalities. We invest continuously in advanced education so our clients receive the most current and effective therapeutic techniques available.

02

Personalization

No two bodies are the same, and no two sessions should be either. Every visit begins with a brief consultation so your therapist understands your goals, concerns, and preferences. Pressure, focus areas, and technique are always adjusted to suit your individual needs that day.

03

Sanctuary

The moment you enter La Belle SPA, the outside world pauses. Our suite is designed to be a genuine escape — warm, quiet, beautifully appointed, and entirely focused on your wellbeing. We take great care in creating an environment where you can truly let go.

04

Community

We are proud neighbors and community members in Shoreline, WA. We support local events, accept insurance to serve clients who need therapeutic massage for medical reasons, and strive to be the kind of business our community can genuinely rely on for health and wellness.

Our Therapists

Licensed, Passionate, Dedicated

Every member of the La Belle SPA team holds an active Washington State license in massage therapy or esthetics. Licensure is just the starting point — our therapists pursue continuing education in specialized modalities, anatomy, and holistic wellness so they can offer you the most informed, effective treatment possible.

What truly sets our team apart is their genuine passion for the craft. Massage therapy and esthetics are not just jobs here — they're vocations. Our therapists are curious, detail-oriented, and deeply committed to your results. They listen carefully, adapt in real time, and take satisfaction in watching clients transform session by session.

We are also one of the few spas in Shoreline to accept Premera Blue Cross insurance, which reflects our commitment to serving clients who need therapeutic massage for legitimate medical and wellness needs — not just luxury relaxation.
