About the Program

La Belle SPA's eyelash extension training is designed for both complete beginners and experienced artists looking to refine their skills. Our instructors are working professionals who perform lash services daily — you'll learn from real experience, not just theory.

We cover everything from lash health and client safety to advanced volume techniques, giving you the complete foundation to launch or elevate your career as a lash artist.

Courses Offered

Classic Lash Certification
One extension per natural lash — the foundation every lash artist must master first.
Volume & Mega Volume
Fan creation, weight ratios, and advanced placement for dramatic, full results.
Lash Health & Safety
Adhesive chemistry, allergic reactions, isolation technique, and client contraindications.
Mapping & Design
Eye shape analysis, curl and length mapping for custom, flattering sets every time.
Hands-On Practice
Work on real models under instructor supervision — not just mannequin heads.
Certificate of Completion
Receive a professional certificate upon passing practical assessment.
