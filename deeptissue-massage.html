What is Deep Tissue Massage?

Deep tissue massage uses slow, deliberate strokes and firm pressure to target the inner layers of muscle and connective tissue. It's the treatment of choice for athletes, office workers, and anyone carrying chronic tension that lighter techniques simply can't reach.

At La Belle SPA, our therapists are expertly trained to work with your body's signals — applying sustained pressure to release trigger points, break up scar tissue, and restore normal muscle function. Communication is key: we customize every session to your comfort level and specific areas of concern.

You may experience mild soreness in the 24-48 hours following a deep tissue session — a sign that healing is occurring. Staying hydrated after your massage supports the body's natural recovery process.
