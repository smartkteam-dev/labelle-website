The Aromatherapy Experience

Aromatherapy massage combines the soothing techniques of Swedish massage with the potent healing properties of therapeutic-grade essential oils. Before your session, our therapist will consult with you about your intentions — whether you seek deep relaxation, mood elevation, stress relief, or immune support — and select the perfect oil blend for your needs.

The oils are diluted in a carrier oil and applied through the massage itself, allowing them to penetrate the skin and enter the bloodstream while their aromatic compounds are inhaled and processed by the limbic system — the brain's emotional center. The combined effect is more profound than either massage or aromatherapy alone.

Popular essential oils in our practice include lavender for relaxation and sleep, eucalyptus for respiratory support and energy, peppermint for focus and muscle relief, frankincense for grounding and anti-inflammation, and rose for emotional healing.

Benefits

Emotional Balance
Essential oils directly stimulate the limbic system to elevate mood and ease anxiety.
Stress Reduction
Combined effect of touch and scent reduces cortisol more effectively than massage alone.
Skin Nourishment
Carrier oils hydrate and soften skin while essential oils provide therapeutic benefits.
Improved Sleep
Lavender and chamomile blends promote deep, restorative sleep for days after your session.
Immune Support
Certain oils like tea tree and eucalyptus have antimicrobial and immune-boosting properties.
Headache Relief
Peppermint and lavender applied to the temples and neck ease tension headaches.
