A 90-Minute Journey for Your Skin

The Luxury Facial is our most complete facial experience — a 90-minute ceremony that leaves no aspect of skin health untouched. Unlike express treatments that focus on one concern, this signature service layers technique upon technique, giving your skin the sustained, expert attention it deserves. Whether you're preparing for a special occasion or committing to a monthly renewal ritual, this is the treatment that delivers the most visible, lasting results.

Your session begins with a thorough double cleanse to remove makeup, sunscreen, and daily buildup. Your therapist then performs a detailed skin analysis under magnification, assessing hydration levels, pore congestion, pigmentation, and texture before customizing the remainder of the treatment to your skin's current state.

The enzyme exfoliation phase is one of the most transformative steps — fruit enzymes gently dissolve the bonds between dead skin cells without the abrasion of mechanical exfoliants, revealing the fresher, more luminous layer beneath. Steam opens pores in preparation for thorough extractions that clear blackheads and congestion without trauma to surrounding tissue.

The lymphatic facial massage portion of the treatment is what truly sets this service apart. Using light, rhythmic strokes along the lymphatic pathways of the face and neck, your therapist reduces puffiness, encourages cellular detoxification, and promotes a natural glow that no product alone can replicate. A customized mask follows, chosen for your specific concerns — whether brightening, calming, or firming.

The treatment concludes with LED light therapy — red and near-infrared wavelengths that penetrate the dermis to stimulate collagen production, reduce inflammation, and accelerate cellular repair. A prescription-grade serum, eye treatment, and SPF finish seal in all the benefits and leave your skin radiant, balanced, and thoroughly renewed.

Ideal For

The Luxury Facial is perfect for anyone preparing for a wedding, reunion, photoshoot, or other special occasion where you want your skin looking its absolute best. It's equally valuable as a monthly maintenance ritual for those serious about long-term skin health. Clients with multiple skin concerns — combination oiliness with dehydration, or aging alongside active breakouts — particularly benefit from the full-protocol approach that addresses every layer of skin function simultaneously.

First-time facial clients often choose the Luxury Facial as an introduction because the comprehensive consultation at the beginning of the session establishes a clear skincare roadmap, giving them expert guidance alongside an exceptional treatment experience.
