Program Overview

La Belle SPA offers hands-on massage therapy training for aspiring practitioners who want to learn from experienced, licensed therapists working in an active spa environment. Our training goes beyond textbooks — you'll work alongside professionals, develop real clinical intuition, and graduate with the confidence to deliver exceptional results.

We keep our program small and personalized by design. Every student receives direct mentorship, individualized feedback, and genuine preparation for a rewarding career in massage therapy.

What You'll Learn

Swedish Massage Technique
Master effleurage, petrissage, friction, tapotement, and vibration with precision and flow.
Deep Tissue & Trigger Points
Learn to safely apply sustained pressure and release chronic muscular tension.
Client Intake & Assessment
Conduct professional consultations, read the body, and customize every session.
Anatomy & Kinesiology
Understand muscles, fascia, and movement patterns that inform every touch decision.
Draping & Professionalism
Maintain client comfort, safety, and trust throughout every session.
Business Foundations
How to build a client base, work with insurance, and operate as a professional therapist.
